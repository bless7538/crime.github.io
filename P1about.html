My Community





Tacarigua

Tacarigua is a town located in the NorthEastern Region of Trinidad and Tobago.
It has a population of 14,000 people. It is known for it's name as well as it's peaceful and clean appearance.
However, there have been reports of a few crimes occurring in this town and therefore we as a community must come together to resolve it.




Solutions to Resolve Crime In the Community


  • Set up a Neighborhood Watch or a community patrol, working with police
  • Report any crime or suspicious activity immediately to the police
  • Create a community anti-violence competition (along with peitions). Include speech, dance, painting, drawing, singing, musical instrument acting, and other creative arts
  • Mentor young people who need positive support from adults -through programs like The Braga Youth Action Plan
